CryptoPulse is a quiet, focused crypto watchlist for Android. Install, pick the coins you care about, drop a widget on your home screen — done.

WHY CRYPTOPULSE

• No account. No login. No email. Open and use.
• No tracking. We collect zero data. No analytics, no crash reports.
• No ads. No paywall. Free and open-source.

HOME-SCREEN WIDGETS

Resizable home-screen widgets adapt to whatever space you give them:
• Small — a single coin, big and readable
• Medium — your top three picks
• Large — your watchlist with 7-day mini charts

Tap any coin on the widget to jump straight into its detail screen.

REAL-TIME PRICES

Inside the app, prices stream in real time over a WebSocket connection to Binance. Watch the row pulse green or red as the price moves — or turn the animation off in Settings if you prefer it still.

MINIMAL CHARTS

Detail screen shows a clean line chart over 1D / 7D / 30D / 1Y. Touch and hold anywhere to read the exact price at that moment. No clutter, no fifteen overlapping indicators.

PRIVACY-FIRST BY DESIGN

Your watchlist is stored on your device only. The app talks only to public crypto APIs (CoinGecko, Binance) to fetch prices. Nothing else.

OPEN SOURCE

Full source code is on GitHub: github.com/visiongem/CryptoPulse
